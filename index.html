!Communicating with home\n!!!!Striking a balance\nYou will need to strike an appropriate balance between the folks back home and your new life abroad. Your family wants to know you are safe and having a good time, and your friends want to keep in touch--so don’t cut them off completely!\n\nAt the same time, be careful not to spend //all// your time chatting back home. Constant contact might be tempting, but will ultimately make culture shock harder to bear and prevent you from experiencing your host country to the fullest. What you report from abroad can seem much more serious to family who aren't around to assess the situation for themselves. <<sidebar "How to Get a Travel Visa">>\nA ''travel visa'' is not a credit card! It's a stamp or sticker placed in your passport that allows you to visit a foreign country.
